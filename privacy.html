By accessing this Website and using its Services you signify that you understand and accept all the provisions of this Policy. If you do not accept any provision of this Policy, please, stop using this Website.

Collected data

The data we collect may include:

  • Your full name;
  • Email;
  • Phone number;
  • Phone number;
  • Social media information.

We do not sell or intend to sell the collected information. Your data will be used only by authorized personnel.

How we use your data

We will use the collected data only for the following purposes:

We will keep your personal information only for as long as it will be necessary to fulfill our contractual obligations. You have a right to request a report containing all information we have about you at any time.

Your rights to your personal information
